Hobbies/Interests

Hiking

Hiking gives me a chance to disconnect from daily life and immerse myself in nature. I enjoy exploring different trails, taking in scenic views, and challenging myself with tougher hikes. There's something rewarding about reaching the peak of a trail and looking back at the journey it took to get there.

Traveling

I love traveling and experiencing new places, cultures, and foods. Exploring different cities and landscapes allows me to gain new perspectives and create lasting memories. Whether it's a road trip to a nearby destination or an international adventure, I always look forward to the next journey.

Basketball

Basketball has always been a passion of mine, and I love following the game at both the professional and casual levels. My favorite team is the Golden State Warriors, and I admire Stephen Curry for his incredible shooting ability and leadership on the court. Whether it's playing a pickup game or watching the Warriors in action, basketball is a sport that always excites me.
